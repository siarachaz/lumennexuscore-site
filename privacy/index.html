Privacy Policy

Lumen Nexus: Core

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in connection with Lumen Nexus: Core.

Lumen Nexus: Core is developed as a solo indie project. Data collection is kept limited and is used only to improve the game, understand technical issues, balance gameplay, and make the early alpha more stable and enjoyable to play.

Anonymous Gameplay Statistics

During gameplay, anonymous gameplay statistics may be sent when a run ends and once when the feedback quest is completed.

These statistics may include gameplay progress, produced and consumed resources, balancing-related values, and technical diagnostic data.

This data does not include your name, email address, local file paths, or any other information intended to identify you.

The data is used only to improve balancing, game feel, progression, stability, and the overall flow of Lumen Nexus: Core.

You can disable the sending of anonymous gameplay statistics at any time in the game under:
Settings > General > Privacy.

Bug Reports and Feedback

When you submit feedback or a bug report, you may choose to include additional anonymized technical diagnostic data by enabling the related toggle.

This optional diagnostic data is only sent if you actively submit feedback or a bug report and choose to include it. No diagnostic data is sent as part of a feedback or bug report without your confirmation.

The optional diagnostic data may include anonymized technical information such as game state, system information, performance statistics, recent gameplay events, and recent errors.

This information is used only to improve Lumen Nexus: Core, investigate reported issues, and reproduce bugs more effectively.

If you write a message in a free-text feedback field, please do not include personal information such as your real name, email address, Discord username, address, phone number, or any other information that could identify you.

Feedback text is only used to understand your report, improve the game, and troubleshoot reported issues.

Third-Party Platforms

Lumen Nexus: Core may be distributed, discussed, or promoted through third-party platforms such as itch.io, Discord, YouTube, Reddit, Twitter/X, Steam, or similar services.

These platforms are operated independently and may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

Anonymous gameplay statistics and diagnostic information are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for development, balancing, troubleshooting, stability improvements, or legal obligations.

Feedback text and bug reports may be kept for as long as they are useful for understanding and resolving reported issues.

Contact

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as development continues, features change, or new distribution platforms are added.

The latest version will always be published on this page.
